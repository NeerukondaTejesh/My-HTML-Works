Fitness & Diet Plans

Custom Training and Diet Plans for Teenagers and Adults

Teenager Training Plan

  • Warm-up: 10 mins jogging/skipping
  • Bodyweight exercises: Push-ups, Squats, Planks
  • Cardio: 15-20 mins cycling or running
  • Cool down: Stretching & deep breathing
  • 3-5 sessions per week

Teenager Diet Plan

  • High protein: Eggs, milk, lean meats
  • Whole grains: Brown rice, oats, wheat
  • Fruits & veggies daily
  • Hydration: At least 2L water/day
  • Avoid: Junk food, sugary sodas

Adult Training Plan

  • Weight training: 4–5 days/week
  • Cardio: 20–30 mins brisk walk/jog
  • Strength: Deadlifts, Bench Press, Squats
  • Stretching & Yoga weekly
  • Rest & recovery: 1–2 days/week

Adult Diet Plan

  • Protein-rich meals (chicken, tofu, lentils)
  • Complex carbs: Brown rice, quinoa, sweet potatoes
  • Healthy fats: Nuts, seeds, olive oil
  • Plenty of fiber & water
  • Limit sugar, alcohol, processed foods
